Start Sending Postcards

What is Postcrossing?

The goal of this project is to allow anyone in India to send and receive postcards from all over the world! The idea is simple: for each postcard you send, you will receive one back from a random postcrosser from somewhere in the world.

How It Works

1. Write a postcard

The first step is to request to send a postcard — we'll give you the address of a random member and a Postcard ID (e.g., IN-12345). Pick a postcard, write a friendly message along with its Postcard ID and the address given. Don't forget the stamps!

2. Post it

Now all you need to do is mail the postcard from your nearest postbox or post office. When your postcard arrives, its recipient will register it using its Postcard ID. This will make you eligible to receive a postcard from another member — and where your postcard will come from is a surprise!

3. Hurray! You've got mail!

One day, you'll check your mailbox and a postcard from someone far away will be waiting for you!

4. Register the postcard

Now it's your turn to register the postcard using its Postcard ID. That's it! You can have several postcards traveling at the same time. The more you send, the more you will receive!
